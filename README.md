### 🇬🇧 English
**Project Overview:**
I engineered a data pipeline to process 2 million rows, identifying why a business with **€509M in sales** only collected **€255M**.

**What I found:**
* **The Bug:** The 50% revenue gap is consistent across Italy, France, and Germany, indicating a systemic technical bug in the payment software rather than a market issue.
* **The Solution:** Fixing the payment module allows the company to recover 50% of its liquidity immediately without additional marketing spend.
* **Data Engineering:** Used Python to handle the high-volume processing and cleaning of the 2M+ record dataset, ensuring data integrity for final analysis.

👉 **The final results and strategic insights are visualized in the interactive dashboard and detailed in the [Business_Strategy_Report.pdf].**

### 🇫🇷 Français
**Aperçu du Projet :**
